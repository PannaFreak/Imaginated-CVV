Anubhav Jha

The Greatest Street Footballer ever Known a.k.a.Panna Freak

Anubhav Jha was born on 23rd December 2002 in the small town of Jharkhand name Dhanbad. He started his journey of Panna Freak while he was pursuing the graduation from School of Open Learning , University of Delhi. He started football when he was 13 year old but after watching the politics in his own school team, he then left his dream of a footballer. After 2 years, he watched a video of a youtuber known Street Panna, he then become fascinated of street football. Now the entire street football community knows him, he inspired so much young talents and take this community several steps forward.
